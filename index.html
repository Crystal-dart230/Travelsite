Come to Kaieteur Falls

Kaieteur Falls, according toTravel Leisure, is a giant waterfall is located on the Potaro River in Kaieteur National Park, within Guyana’s region of the Amazon rainforest. Imagine water dropping from a vertical rock face four times the highest point of Niagara Falls at 23,400 cubic feet per second — and, contrary to its North American counterpart, Kaieteur is all natural.

Georgetown Guyana

According to Culture Trip,For a dose of civilization before heading out to the remote savanna or jungle forest, the capital of Guyana, Georgetown offers a slice of lively urban life. Colonial buildings dominate the town’s architecture, telling the story of the city’s Dutch and British heritage. Meanwhile, its lively markets, spattering of museums and handful of good restaurants make the city an entertaining weekend stay or stopover.
